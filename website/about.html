About Ion

Ion is a community-driven Android ROM that prioritizes privacy, customization, and performance. Built from open-source components, Ion provides users with choices and transparency.

Our mascot (a purple robot with a headband) represents friendliness and openness.

Vision

To offer a modern mobile experience that respects users and gives them control over their devices.
