# Open Source Alternatives to Google Services

- Google Play Services: microG
- Google Play Store: F-Droid
- Google Maps: OpenStreetMap (OsmAnd, Magic Earth)
- Google Search: DuckDuckGo, Searx
- Google Drive: Nextcloud
- Gmail: K-9 Mail
- Google Calendar: Etar
- Google Photos: PhotoPrism, Simple Gallery
- Google Chrome: Firefox, Bromite
- Google Contacts: DAVx⁵
- Google Messages: Signal, Element
- Google Keep: Joplin, Standard Notes
- Google Assistant: Mycroft AI
- Google News: Feedly, Flym
- Google Fit: Gadgetbridge

## Integration Plan
- Replace default apps with open source alternatives
- Integrate microG for Play Services compatibility
- Document all replacements in user guide
